Heroes Minigame

Instructions:
  1. Check the heros out.
  2. Hover over their abilities pictures to see what they do.
  3. Select on one of the heros. The computer will choose one too.
  4. Click "Start Fight" to begin the battle.
  5. Open the battlelog to see which hero won and fight deatils per turn.
  6. Click restart if you want to replay the game.
  7. Have fun!

Sylveon

Class: Sprite

Sprites have a glass canon build, which means that they deal a lot of damage but are not very tanky.

Abilities:
Heart of Fire

Sylveon deals a power blast of 100 normal attack damage.
Sass

Sylveon has a 50% chance to deal extra 50 normal damage attack per turn.
Broken Heart

When entering battle, Sylveon injures herself by 500, starting with only 1000 hp.
Angular Wings

Sylveon has a chance of 50% of dodging an attack by flying away.
HP: 1500 - 500 Attack: 150 Can fly Extra damage

Dragonair

Class: Dragon

Dragons can fly to dodge attacks and are generally very tanky thanks to their size and scaley skin.

Abilities:
Snek Attack

Dragonair uses his horn to deal 50 normal attack damage.
Don't React

When attacked, Dragonair has a 50% chance to fly away and avoid incoming damage.
Tailwind

Dragonair uses his tail as a shield, taking only 80% damage dealt.
Big Drake

Thanks to his big size, Dragoinair has extra 600 hp to the base 1500 hp.
HP: 1500 + 600 Attack: 50 Can shield self Can fly

Smoliv

Class: Dwarf

Dwarf Heroes are very smol but powerful so don't get fooled by their size.

Abilities:
He attacc

Smoliv throws olives at the enemy, dealing 100 normal attack damage.
He protecc

When attacked, Smoliv absorbs 20% incoming damage with his smol Olive.
Can't Stop Smoliv

When attacked, Smoliv has a 30% chance to heal for 10% of his initial health.
Extra Virgin Olive Oil

When entering battle, Smoliv doubles his size, also doubling his health.
HP: 1000+ 1000 Attack: 100 Can heal self Can shield self

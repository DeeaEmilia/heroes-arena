Heroes Minigame

Instructions:

  1. Click select on one of the heros.
  2. The computer will choose one too.
  3. Click "Start Fight" to begin the battle.
  4. Open the battlelog to see which hero won.
  5. Click restart if you wanna replay the game.
  6. Have fun!

Sylveon

Class: Sprite
Abilities:
Heart of Fire

Sylveon deals a power blast of 100 normal attack damage.
Sass

Sylveon has a 50% chance to deal extra 50 normal damage attack per turn.
Broken Heart

When entering battle, Sylveon injures herself by 500, starting with only 1000 hp.
Angular Wings

Sylveon has a chance of 50% of dodging an attack by flying away.
HP: 1500 - 500 Attack: 150 Can fly Extra damage

Dragonair

Class: Dragon
Abilities:
Snek Attack

Dragonair uses his horn to deal 50 normal attack damage.
Don't React

When attacked, Dragonair has a 50% chance to fly away and avoid incoming damage.
Tailwind

Dragonair uses his tail as a shield, taking only 80% damage dealt.
Big Drake

Thanks to his big size, Dragoinair has extra 600 hp to the base 1500 hp.
HP: 1500 + 600 Attack: 50 Can shield self Can fly
